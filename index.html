Individual Employee Credit

For employers interested in incorporating Intripid into a performance incentive and/or recognition program, Intripid offers the ability to add credit to individual employee accounts.
Credit can be applied toward fees associated with Step-2 of the Intripid process – e.g., hiring a travel advisor for personalized, low-level trip planning services (e.g., booking golf tee times) or paying for other trip expenses.
Since credit cannot be “cashed out” by employees, this is both a great reward and a powerful, use-or-lose mechanism to encourage employees to use their PTO and engage in restorative leisure travel.
